Tá uaigh ag fanacht.

moon o'er mountain
her fog crawls into the valley
where we make our bed
nights oft-wished for
drunk deep between the two of us
morning never comes
your love stays under sheets
locked in time with mine
no moon nor mountain hold the key
for we gave it to the water
she rolled it through her hands
where iron becomes rust
